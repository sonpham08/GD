Trang 1/50

From: Sharon Dewey
To: All staff
Date: May 3
Subject: Leave policy
The revised leave policy will come into effect as of May 4.
To summarize the updated policy, which was e-mailed to you all on April 15, all staff will now have to request leave no less than two calendar weeks before the date upon which the proposed leave commences. Requests must be submitted via the company intranet by clicking on ‘Absence Management’, and then ‘Submit Leave Request’. Once entered into the system, request forms will be forwarded automatically to your line manager and department manager (where these differ).
We trust that these arrangements are clear. If not, feel free to get in touch with your Human Resources contact.
